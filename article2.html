Multiple missing persons are found as an illegal
experimentation facility is busted

Author: David Arroyo, 4:30 pm

After years of unresolved cases, the Pasay Disappearances Case has been reopened and solved, uncovering an illegal experimentation facility beneath Villareal Medical Hospital.

More than 25 individuals were rescued from the site after police received a tip from Detective Victor Williams of the city station. Authorities also found multiple bodies, including Olivia Villareal, the wife of the hospital owner.

Liam Villareal, the mastermind, is now in police custody. He is expected to face charges such as unethical human experimentation, third-degree murder, kidnapping, assault, and others as the investigation continues.

His accomplice, Marshall Constantine, has admitted his involvement and will cooperate fully with authorities.

“Ito yung pinaka-nakakakilabot na kasong hinawakan ko… Hirap rin isipin kung anong mga nangyari sa mga biktima. Pero rest assured, gagawin namin lahat para mabigyan ng hustisya yung mga taong nabiktima ng ospital," said Victor Williams, the lead detective. He also mentioned in an interview that the victims will receive medical and psychological care before being reunited with their families.
