Prank gone wrong — 10-year-old killed in local diner

Author: Casey Montoya, 2:31 pm

On the morning of November 12th, a child’s birthday celebration at the popular “Fredbear’s Family Diner” turned into a nightmare when a group of local teenagers forced a young boy into the mouth of the star animatronic.

The twisted prank gone wrong ended in tragedy when the robotic performer crushed the boy’s head, killing him instantly. The four young men are exempt from criminal liability but will be subjected to an intervention program.

But sources say that the victim’s older brother led the brutal act and may be more liable, thus potentially subject to the appropriate proceedings in accordance with Republic Act No. 9344.

“It’s a tragic incident, especially on a day when a child is expected to be happiest. Fazbear Entertainment extends our deepest condolences to the victim’s family and promises to support the investigation.” Ralph Cornellius, the current Fazbear Entertainment representative, stated.

Trigger Warning: Blood. Photo of the animatronic performer after recovery.

The victim’s family has yet to respond to our request for an interview.
